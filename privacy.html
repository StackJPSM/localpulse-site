Privacy Policy for Evoora Digital

Last Updated: April 2026

At Evoora, we are built by hand to help other hands. That commitment to partnership includes being transparent about how we handle the data of our clients and their customers. This policy outlines our practices for digital advertising, web development, and SEO services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our "Build + Grow" services, we collect the following:

  • Client Information: Name, business email, phone number, and physical address for local campaign targeting and billing.
  • Payment Data: We collect payments through secure third-party processors (e.g., Stripe/PayPal). Evoora does not store full credit card numbers on our own servers.
  • Project Assets: Logos, images, and brand details provided for flyers, web development, and social media content.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns collected via cookies on our website and the websites we build for clients to improve SEO and user experience.

2. Digital Services & Third-Party Advertising

When we manage campaigns on your behalf, data is processed by third-party platforms:

  • Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)
  • Snapchat Ads (Snap Pixel attribution)
  • Google Ads & SEO (Analytics + Search Console)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Build and host custom websites
  • Run targeted digital ad campaigns
  • Improve SEO performance
  • Provide monthly reports and updates

4. Data Retention & Security

We retain client information for the duration of the service agreement and a reasonable period afterward for legal and support purposes. We protect your data as if it were our own.

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request corrections
  • Opt out of marketing communications or tracking
